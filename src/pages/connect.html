Hi there,
If you've found this page, hopefully you're wanting to know more about me as a person. If you're in the wrong spot and are looking for more information about me in the workplace, check out my 'me' (above) or 'about' (below) section.

So... a bit about me. I was actually born in India, and was adopted by an American family - my parents were both computer engineers but until recently, I actually had no idea what that entailed. I just knew they were very smart. My dad had an IBM Thinkpad in the 90s which was pretty high tech for the early days and I absolutely loved how fast the mouse would move if I vigorously held the little red button in the middle of the keyboard.

Anyways, fast-forward to now and I'm always the one in the group who can press a few keys/buttons on anything electronic and voilà - the item is working again! I always liked computers and their advanced capabilities so I think it's fitting that I've taken the route in synthesizing design and computers, in whatever way that may be. I have a passion for clean and minimum design when I create which is probably also a testament to my lifestyle. I also enjoy challenging thought-processes and logic, which may have come from my general childhood.

Since moving to Australia from Washington D.C. (USA), I've been lucky enough to gain a greater appreciation for the general outdoors. I enjoy spending time in the sun and near the ocean. I try to live a mindful and purposeful daily life, with balance and energy. I spend a lot of time doing pilates and riding horses (when I can)! My husband and I also love to travel and experiencing new places. A fun fact about me - I've gone cage-floor diving with great white sharks off the coast of South Australia!

If you'd like to know more or think we'd be a good team for a project in the professional domain, feel free to click the contact button and send me a message!
